  Stormthistles are a blue flower distantly related to Lilacs.
<~><~>  They are found in Mountainous Jungles among the Jungle Trees and Beanstalks.
<~><~>  The Stormthistle is the herb best suited for Electromancy magic. Though it is most useful when it is dried and crushed.
<~><~>  Stormthistle Seeds can be crafted from Stormthistles and can be grown on Farmland.
<~><~>  Stormthistles can be mashed into Storm Water or Cyan Dye.
