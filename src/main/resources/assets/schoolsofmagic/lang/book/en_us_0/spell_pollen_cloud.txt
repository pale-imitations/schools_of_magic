  This spell channels natural energy into a bolt of magic that upon impact creates a cloud of disorienting pollen. <~><~> Any creature that enters this 3 block radius cloud will be afflicted with sneezing for about 6 seconds. <~><~>  Everytime a creature sneezes it briefly forgets who it is attacking, some nature magic creatures, such as the Dryads, are immune to this effect. <~><~>  This cloud will last for about 20 seconds.
