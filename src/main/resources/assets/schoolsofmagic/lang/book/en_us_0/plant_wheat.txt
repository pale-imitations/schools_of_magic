  Wheat is a common crop cultivated by the earliest humans from the grass of the plains.
<~><~>  Wheat can be grown from seeds found by breaking Grass.
<~><~>  Dried, Crushed Wheat can be used for Transfiguration magic.
<~><~>  Wheat is also used in a variety of recipes including Cake, Bread, Cookies, and Herbal Twine.
