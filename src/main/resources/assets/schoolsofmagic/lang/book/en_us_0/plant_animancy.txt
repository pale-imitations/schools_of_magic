  Mandrakes, also called Mandragora, are a rooty plant distantly related to Nightshade.
<~><~>  They are found in Mountainous Jungles and Sinister Swamps among the ferns.
<~><~>  Mandrakes are connected to Animancy magic. Though it is most useful when it is dried and crushed.
<~><~>  Mandrake Roots can be harvested and planted on Farmland, it can also be placed on Mud.
<~><~>  Mandrake Roots can be mashed into Mud Man Juice or Mandrake Pulp, both useful in potion making.
