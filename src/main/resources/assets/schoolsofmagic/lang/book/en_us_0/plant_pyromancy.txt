  Pyracantha, also called Firethorn, is a prickly bush in the Rose family.
<~><~>  It is native to Autumn Woods and Strange Hills biomes growing among the Bastion Trees and Beanstalks.
<~><~>  Pyracantha is the herb best suited for Pyromancy magic. Though it is most useful when it is dried and crushed.
<~><~>  Fireberries can be harvested from Pyracantha and planted on Farmland to grow more of this herb.
<~><~>  Fireberry Juice, a valuable ingredient in potion making, can be crafted by mashing Fireberries in a Mortar and Pestle.
