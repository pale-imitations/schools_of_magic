  Grave Grass, also called Corpse Root, is a wispy brown grass that naturally grows over a creature's grave.
<~><~>  They are found in Sinister Swamps, Banded Deserts, Desert Canyons, and Desert Uplands.
<~><~>  Grave Grass has a natural affinity for Necromantic magic. Though it and it’s strange roots are most useful when they are dried and crushed.
<~><~>  Grave Root can be harvested from Grave Grass and can be grown on Farmland. It can also be planted on Mud.
<~><~>  Grave Root can be mashed into Dead Man’s Blood or Grave Root Pulp, both are valuable ingredients.
