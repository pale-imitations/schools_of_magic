  These dark red speckled mushrooms are found in Acolyte Woods.
<~><~>  These mushrooms can be planted on Wood, Dirt, Grass, Mycelium, and Podzol, or on any solid block in low light levels.
<~><~>  Redcap Mushroom Heads will regrow their stalks over time becoming larger over time. Both the mushroom head and mushroom stalks are a valuable potion ingredient.
<~><~>  Redcap Mushroom Heads can be crafted into Redcap Spores, which can be planted and grown.
