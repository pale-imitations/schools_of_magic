  Clovers are an herb distantly related to Beanstalks.
<~><~>  They are found in Ancient Taigas, Ancient Forests, and Ancient Dark Forests, and Ash Woods.
<~><~>  Clovers are rich in Geomancy magic. Though it is most useful when it is dried and crushed.
<~><~>  Clovers Seeds can be crafted from Clovers and can be grown on Farmland.
<~><~>  Occasionally Four Leaf Clovers can be found in Clovers, these useful items have entirely different properties.
