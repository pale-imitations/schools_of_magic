  This spell creates a spell circle which emits an illusory sounds with an active radius of 20 blocks and which lasts 2 minutes.<~><~>  By right-clicking the spells you can toggle the sound which is emitted.<~><~>  The spell can imitate a whispering noise footsteps, Creepers, Endermen, Zombies, Skeletons, Villages, Blazes, Witches, Cats, Wolves, a heartbeat, Frogs, or tapping.<~><~>  When imitating footsteps, Mobs within the radius become attracted to the sound and when imitating a villager, Zombies will be attracted.<~><~>  When imitating a Cat Creepers are repelled and when imitating a Wolf, Skeletons are repelled.
