  Wormwood, known by many names including Mugwort, is a ghostly green plant distantly related to Brittle Bush.
<~><~>  They are found in Ancient Birch Forests and the River Lands.
<~><~>  Wormwood is the herb best suited for Spectromancy magic. Though it is most useful when it is dried and crushed.
<~><~>  Wormwood Seeds can be harvested from Wormwood in a crafting table and can be grown on Farmland and planted on Mycelium or Soul Sand.
<~><~>  Wormwood can be mashed into Absinthe, a valuable ingredient with mild hallucinatory properties.
