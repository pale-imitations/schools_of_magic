  Venus Flytraps are carnivorous plants that attract insects and which damage Spiders.
<~><~>  They can be found in a variety of swamps such as River Lands, Sinister Swamps, Wetlands, and Mountainous Jungles.
<~><~>  Venus Flytraps have a connection to Abjuration magic. Though they are most useful when they are dried and crushed.
