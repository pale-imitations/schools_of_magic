  Lithops are a strange magenta succulent that is believed to have come from the End Dimension.
<~><~>  They are found in Vermillion Grove growing among the Vermilion Trees and the Mallow.
<~><~>  Lithops are rich in Chaotic magic, though they are most useful when they are dried and crushed.
<~><~>  Their seeds can be crafted by putting a Lithop in a crafting table and can be cultivated on Farmland and can also be planted on End Stone.
<~><~>  Lithops can be crushed in a Mortar and Pestle to make Lithop Pulp.
