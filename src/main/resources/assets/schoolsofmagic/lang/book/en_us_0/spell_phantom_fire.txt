  This spell creates the illusion of a flame on the block.<~><~>  This spell causes no damage and leaves no lasting effect, however it is very useful as a distraction and as an illusory obstacle.<~><~>  Phantom Fire cannot be put out by regular means, but it will eventually naturally dispell.<~><~>  However, like real fire, placing Netherrack beneath Phantom Fire will allow it to last forever.
