  Lilacs are a tall purple flower distantly related to the Acolyte Tree.
<~><~>  They can be found in Plains and Flower Biomes.
<~><~>  Lilacs have a connection to Divination magic. Though they are most useful when they are dried and crushed.
<~><~>  They can be cultivated by right clicking them with Bone Meal.
<~><~>  Lilacs can be mashed into Magenta Dye in a Mortar and Pestle.
