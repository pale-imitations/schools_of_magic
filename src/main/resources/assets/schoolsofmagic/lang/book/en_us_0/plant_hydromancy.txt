  Lotuses are a blue flower closely related to Water Lilies.
<~><~>  These flowers grow naturally in the waters of the Mountainous Jungles.
<~><~>  The Lotus is perfectly suited for Hydromancy magic. Though it is most useful when it is dried and crushed.
<~><~>  Lotus Seeds can be crafted from Lotuses and can be grown on Water with a block of Dirt below or on Mud.
<~><~>  Lotuses can be mashed into Lake Dew, a valuable potion ingredient.
