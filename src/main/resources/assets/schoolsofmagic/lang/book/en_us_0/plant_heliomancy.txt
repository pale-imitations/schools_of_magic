  Marigolds are a bright orange flower related to Sunflowers.
<~><~>  They are found in Banded Deserts, Desert Canyons, and Desert Uplands growing among the Barrel Cactuses and Jubilee Trees.
<~><~>  Marigolds are rich in Heliomancy magic. Though it is most useful when it is dried and crushed.
<~><~>  Their seeds can be crafted by putting a Marigold in a crafting table and can be cultivated on Farmland.
<~><~>  Marigolds can also be crafted into Orange Dye in a Mortar and Pestle.
