  Lavender is an aromatic purple flower related to Sage.
<~><~>  These flowers grow naturally in very cold Ancient biomes and in the Elder Grove.
<~><~>  Lavender is connected to Hieromancy magic, though they are most useful when it is dried and crushed.
<~><~>  Lavender Seeds can be crafted from Lavender and can be planted on Farmland.
<~><~>  Lavender can be crushed into Purple Dye in a Mortar and Pestle.
